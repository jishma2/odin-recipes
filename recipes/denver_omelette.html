Denver Omelette

A great breakfast meal!

Contains eggs,butter, ham, diced onions, diced green bell pepper, cheddar cheese, and pepper

Ingredients

  • 3 large eggs
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • 1/4 cup diced smoked ham
  • 2 tablespoons diced onion
  • 2 tablespoons diced green bell pepper
  • 1/3 cup shredded cheese
  • 1 pinch black pepper

Steps

  1. Beat eggs until combined
  2. Melt butter on high, add ham, onion, and pepper, sprinkle salt and pepper.Cook med-heat 5 minutes
  3. Reduce heat to med-low, pour in eggs, use clock method to spread evenly
  4. Sprinkle cheese and pepper over omelette. Serve after folding
