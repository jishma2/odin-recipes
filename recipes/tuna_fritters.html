BBQ Tuna Fritters

A healthy lunch!

Tuna,egg,oats,BBQ sauce, onions, hot pepper sauce

Ingredients

  • 1 can light tuna, drained
  • 1 egg
  • 2/3 cup quick-cooking oats
  • 3 tablespoons barbeque sauce
  • 3 tablespoons chopped green onion
  • 1/2 teaspoon hot pepper sauce
  • 1 pinch salt

Steps

  1. Stir tuna, eggs, and oats
  2. Mix in barbeque sauce, onion, hot pepper, and salt
  3. Med heat, spoon into pan and flatten
  4. Cook until brown each side, ~3 minutes
