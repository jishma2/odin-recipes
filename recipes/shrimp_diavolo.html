Shrimp Fra Diavolo

An impressive dinner!

Contains linguini, shrimp, garlic, tomatos, garlic, red pepper flakes, red wine

Ingredients

  • 1 package linguini
  • 1 pound cooked and peeled shrimp
  • 8 cloves garlic
  • 2 cans diced tomatoes
  • 1 can crushed tomatoes with garlic
  • 2 teaspoons crushed red pepper flakes
  • 2 oz red wine

Steps

  1. Place tomatoes, garlic, red pepper, and wine in a sauce pan, simmer over low for 30 min
  2. cook linguini, when pasta almost done, drain shrimp and place in colander. Drain pasta over shrimp.
  3. Transfer pasta to large sreving bowl and toss in sauce
